THE PORTUGAL GOLDEN VISA ISN'T OVER

US citizens can apply for a Portuguese Golden Visa by investing €500,000 in a qualifying fund.

WHAT IS THE PORTUGAL GOLDEN VISA?

Very popular with American citizens, the Portuguese programme grants residency for you and qualifying family members
(spouse, dependent children & parents over 65), to work, live and study in Portugal. After holding your investment for six
years you can apply for a Portuguese (EU) passport, allowing you to work and live in any EU country.

PORTUGAL GV FUNDS

One way to apply for a Portuguese Golden Visa in 2024 is by investing in a qualifying fund. We can connect you with
exclusive fund options that allow you to invest from €500,000. The companies that benefit from the capitalisation must
be at least five years old at the time that the investment is made and at least 60% of the investments are realised in a
commercial company with its HQ in Portugal.

PORTUGAL GOLDEN VISA BENEFITS

EU passport within 6 years

Instant free travel across the 27 Schengen countries

Low visitation requirement - av. average, 7 days per year

Dependent family can join your application
